Privacy Policy

The privacy policy applicable to this website

Please note that this privacy policy applies to this website only, and not to the non-web activities of the Old Foleyans’ Association. The privacy policy applicable to this website complies with the EU’s General Data Protection Regulation, effective from 25th May 2018.

Collection and / or storage of personal data

This website does not collect personal data from visitors or customers, or store or process such data, except for voluntarily provided comments which are handled as described in the ‘Comments’ section below.

Specifically this website does not use cookies, Google Analytics, Google Fonts, or any other technology that collects either anonymous data or data relating to identifiable individuals.

Comments

Comments voluntarily provided by visitors may be stored on this website and publicly displayed indefinitely.

Anybody who has made a comment published on this website can ask for it to be amended or removed, and the Old Foleyans’ Association will act on any such request within a reasonable length of time.

The Old Foleyans’ Association reserves the right to edit comments, provided that the original meaning and spirit of the comments are not changed.

Please note that comments made using the form provided on this website are sent to a third-party service (run by ‘formspree.io’) that then forwards it to us by e-mail.

The processing of comments by the the third-party service is subject to the privacy policy of ‘formspree.io’, which is different from that of the Old Foleyans’ Association.

Links to other websites with different privacy policies

This website contains links to other websites each of which may have its own privacy policy that does involve the collection, storage, or processing of personal data.

The Old Foleyans’ Association is not responsible for the content or policies of any of the third-party websites to which links are provided for your convenience.

A brief summary of the privacy policy applicable to non-web activities

Please note that this is just a short summary of the privacy policy that applies to the non-web activities of the Old Foleyans’ Association, which is available as a printed document from the data controller who can be contacted using the details provided on the Contact Us page. The privacy policy applicable to the non-web activities of the Old Foleyans’ Association complies with the EU’s General Data Protection Regulation, effective from 25th May 2018.

The Old Foleyans’ Association does collect, store and use personal data

In the normal performance of their non-web duties the Old Foleyans’ Association does collect, store and use personal data, but always requests written authorisation before doing so. The Old Foleyans’ Association fully respects member privacy and will never pass personal data to any third party, except as required by law.

Privacy policy amendments

The Old Foleyans’ Association reserves the right to amend the above-mentioned privacy policies from time to time.
